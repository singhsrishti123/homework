My City

Introduction

Hajipur, the district headquarters of Vaishali in Bihar, India, is a city steeped in history, a hub for education and industry,
and a gateway to the state capital Patna via the Mahatma Gandhi Bridge.

Art and Culture of my city

Hajipur is sanctified with traditions which have incessantly progressed throughout the last 2500 years of evident history. Situated on the river Ganga,
it possesses its own golden splendor of its arts and traditions and the city has observed several ups and down from its commencement to its existing.
The residents encompass diverse sects of natives who have come to the city and eventually settled down in Hajipur.

Food of Hajipur

The cuisine of the city replicate the custom brought along the times past and is fairly diverse. The Mughal and Central Asia food,
brought down with the overseas intruders during the medieval period is also found in the city. Rice figure out to be the staple food,
eaten in blend with lentils, vegetables, and pickles. People have a preference for Samosa-Kachaudi with Jalebis in the mealtime.
